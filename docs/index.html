FAQ

What is this website for?

It's hard to know when Prelims and Challenges are being held on MTGO. This site, an outgrowth of the @mtgoupdate family of Twitter accounts, provides that information to you in your home time zone.

Prelims and Challenges? What about Showcase Challenges, MOCS Opens, RCQs, etc.?

No, at least for the moment. Prelims and Challenges occur at the same time every week, so to get them right I just have to teach the website what a week is. All those other events happen much more rarely, so I'd have to teach it all about calendars to include them.

Complete information for these special events, in three time zones, can be found on Twitter @mtgoupdate, @mtgoupdate_eu, and @mtgoupdate_jp.

I live outside the United States and am about to change my clocks for Daylight Saving Time, or the US is changing its clocks soon but I live elsewhere and will not be changing mine. Is the information on this page accurate for after the change?

No. Check back again after the clocks change.
