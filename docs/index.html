FAQ

What is this website for?

It's hard to know when scheduled events are being held on MTGO. This site provides that information to you in your home time zone.

I'm viewing this page right before the US changes its clocks for Daylight Saving Time (but I won't be changing mine). Are events that take place after the switch listed at the correct times?

Yes! The US's DST policy is simple and predictable, so it's no trouble to incorporate it.

I'm viewing this page right before my country changes its clocks for DST. Are events that take place after the switch listed at the correct times?

No. Anticipating when your locality enters or exits DST would require me to obsessively monitor hundreds of legislatures for changes in policy and to take a side in numerous territorial disputes around the world.

Check back again after the clocks change.
